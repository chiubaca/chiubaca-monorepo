## 🧰 Spotlight

On the AI topic, I've been using **[Herdr ](https://herdr.dev/)** a whole bunch and loving it. If you've struggld with tmux in the past but want persistence terminals that is also AI agent aware. This tool is awesome. It works with opencode , claude code, pii. Pretty much any agent. It will track if the agent is running or blocked and chime you. No need to remember any hotkeys as its a tui that mouse-first.

What really neat about it is that it ships with a skill, so you can ask your ai agent to control herder , spin up agents on different panes and they can even communicate with each other. Its really cool workflow
